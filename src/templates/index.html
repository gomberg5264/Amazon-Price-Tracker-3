Buy your favourite products on Amazon, in your budget.

Welcome to the Amazon Price Tracker. With this app, you can track the price of your favourite products on Amazon. Just follow the four simple steps to get started.

Step 1:
Create an account, and log in.
Step 2:
In the home screen, click on the add item button.
Step 3:
Enter the url of the amazon product page, and the desired price
Step 4:
Get notified by email as soon as the price hits your desired price.
